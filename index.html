Hi! This is the website for THE WIFE OF THEM ALL (2022), a film by Rebecca Close.

This site playfully uses 'local storage' (similar to cookies) to draw attention to the complicity of the viewer in creating meaning & value via the screen

which is one of the main themes of the film

This is not quite a cookies ‘accept/reject’ button, just sharing information about the tools used.

I’m ok with this.

The Wife of Them All (2022) follows a family based accross continents as they traverse and move beyond borders, mad bureacracy and precarious work. Anarchic linking between clay animation; time-lapse microscopy and feminist video art in a project that contextualises the booming fertility market in relation to the migratory control system.
The film premiered at the 27th LesGaiCineMad Festival and the legendary International Short Film Festival Oberhausen in its 69th edition, with the support of HAMACA Moving Image Platform.
